Prayer Answered, Child Dedicated

Hannah's Prayer

In 1 Samuel 1:1-2:11, we witness the power of prayer and the importance of dedicating our children to God. Hannah, who is barren, pours out her heart to the Lord in prayer, and God answers by giving her a son. She then dedicates Samuel to the Lord's service, demonstrating true faith and commitment.

In her deep anguish Hannah prayed to the Lord, weeping bitterly. And she made a vow, saying, "Lord Almighty, if you will only look on your servant's misery and remember me, and not forget your servant but give her a son, then I will give him to the Lord for all the days of his life, and no razor will ever be used on his head." 1 Samuel 1:10-11

Hannah's prayer demonstrates remarkable faith. She doesn't just ask for a son for her own sake, but promises to give him back to the Lord. Her prayer is answered, and she keeps her vow, bringing Samuel to the tabernacle to serve the Lord. This demonstrates the power of prayer and the importance of keeping our promises to God.

Dedication to God

Hannah's dedication of Samuel to the Lord demonstrates the importance of dedicating our lives and our children to God. Just as Hannah gave Samuel to the Lord, we are called to give ourselves to Christ. Samuel's birth through prayer also points to how Christ was born in answer to the prayers of God's people for a Savior.

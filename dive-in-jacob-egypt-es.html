Conclusion: God's Faithful Promise

The story of Jacob's family moving to Egypt shows God's faithfulness to His promises. He promised to make Abraham a great nation, and He was fulfilling that promise. This points to God's faithfulness in fulfilling all His promises through Christ.

God's promise to bless all nations through Abraham finds its ultimate fulfillment in Christ. The church, made up of people from every nation, is the fulfillment of that promise. And just as Joseph provided for his family, so Christ provides for His people, the church.

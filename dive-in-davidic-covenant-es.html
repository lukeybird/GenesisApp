La Promesa de Dios a David

In 2 Samuel 7:1-29, Dios hace un pacto eterno con David, prometiendo que su reino durar√° para siempre. Este pacto es una de las promesas m√°s significativas de las Escrituras, apuntando directamente a Cristo, el Hijo de David que cumple esta promesa.

"The Lord declares to you that the Lord himself will establish a house for you: When your days are over and you rest with your ancestors, I will raise up your offspring to succeed you, your own flesh and blood, and I will establish his kingdom. He is the one who will build a house for my Name, and I will establish the throne of his kingdom forever." 2 Samuel 7:11-13

God's promise to David is remarkable. Instead of allowing David to build a house (temple) for God, God promises to build a house (dynasty) for David. This covenant promises an eternal kingdom through David's line, which finds its ultimate fulfillment in Christ.

El Reino Eterno

The Davidic covenant promises an eternal kingdom that will never end. This promise points forward to Christ, who establishes the eternal kingdom of God and reigns forever as the Son of David.

God's promise that David's throne will be established forever is fulfilled in Christ. While human kings come and go, Christ reigns eternally. The Davidic covenant finds its ultimate fulfillment in Jesus, who is both the Son of David and the Son of God.

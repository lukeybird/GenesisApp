God's Judgment on Sin and False Gods

The ten plagues in Exodus 7:14-12:30 demonstrate God's power over the false gods of Egypt. Each plague targeted a specific Egyptian deity, showing that the Lord alone is God. This judgment on false gods points to Christ's victory over all powers and authorities, and His ultimate judgment on sin.

Having disarmed principalities and powers, He made a public spectacle of them, triumphing over them in it. Colossians 2:15

Just as God demonstrated His supremacy over Egypt's gods through the plagues, so Christ demonstrated His supremacy over all spiritual powers through the cross. The plagues were a judgment on idolatry and false worship; the cross was the ultimate judgment on sin and the powers of darkness. Through Christ's death and resurrection, all false gods and spiritual powers are disarmed and defeated.
